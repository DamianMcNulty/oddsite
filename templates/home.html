About OddBird

OddProjects

We built [Susy](http://susy.oddbird.net) for web designers like ourselves, and we are core contributors to [Compass](http://compass-style.org), [Django](http://djangoproject.com) and other open-source projects.

These days we’re building the UI for [Mozilla’s](http://mozilla.org) new [Test Case Management System](http://cc.oddsites.net) and a parcel-mapping tool for [The Providence Plan](http://provplan.org).

OddBrothers

OddBird is three brothers who thrive on freely shared ideas and playful collaboration, crossing disciplines, breaking boundaries and eating our dessert with the soup spoon.

Carl is a [coder](http://github.com/carljm), [Pythonista](http://www.python.org), and [Django](http://www.djangoproject.com) core developer. When he’s not chasing a more elegant algorithm, he chases frisbees, hockey pucks, and a four-year-old son.

Eric is a [multimedia artist](http://eric.andmeyer.com) working with [words](http://greengreenmud.com/), [bodies](http://www.vicioustrap.com/), [music](http://www.ravenjane.com/), images, lights, film, [code](http://www.compass-style.org/), and many many [other artists](http://eric.andmeyer.com/people/). He's probably starting a new project right now.

Jonny is a [front-end developer](http://github.com/jgerigmeyer), project manager, and inconsistent [college philosophy professor](http://www.goshen.edu/~jonam/). He likes his frisbees thrown long, his students smart-alecky, and his scotch served neat.

OddProcess

We use an [**iterative** process], deploying and integrating new functionality frequently and aiming to deliver usable product launches as early and often as possible.

We encourage clients to stay closely **connected** with the daily progress, offering frequent feedback and re-prioritization via [Pivotal Tracker](http://pivotaltracker.com), our project management tool.

In all of our projects we work exclusively with the open web stack: **accessible** semantic HTML5 markup, unobtrusive Javascript, and advanced CSS3 styling. On the server side we are a Python/Django shop, and have extensive Django experience.
